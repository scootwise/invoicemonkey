# Local Business Prospects — Invoice Parser
## Roseville/Rocklin/Lincoln CA Area

---

## **Criteria**
- Use QuickBooks (or similar accounting)
- Receive regular vendor invoices (weekly+)
- Small-to-medium size (owner-operator or small team)
- Likely overwhelmed by paperwork

---

## **The List**

### 1. **Coffee Shops / Cafes**
- **Target:** Bloom Coffee (Roseville), Fourscore Coffee (Rocklin), local independent cafes
- **Pain:** Multiple food/beverage vendors, daily invoices
- **Approach:** Morning visit, buy coffee, ask about bookkeeping pain
- **Hook:** "How long does invoice entry take you each week?"

### 2. **Auto Repair Shops**
- **Target:** Local shops on Douglas Blvd, Rocklin Rd
- **Pain:** Parts invoices from multiple distributors (NAPA, O'Reilly, etc.)
- **Approach:** Drop in during slow afternoon
- **Hook:** "Do you have someone typing all these parts invoices into QB?"

### 3. **Property Management Companies**
- **Target:** RNB Property Management (already on invoice!), local PM firms
- **Pain:** Maintenance invoices from contractors, multiple properties
- **Approach:** LinkedIn connect, reference existing workflow
- **Hook:** "I noticed you get invoices like this one — want to automate it?"

### 4. **Restaurant/Bars**
- **Target:** Independent restaurants on Pacific St, Vernon St (Old Roseville)
- **Pain:** Daily food/beverage deliveries, invoices pile up
- **Approach:** Visit before lunch rush (10am), ask for manager
- **Hook:** "What happens if invoices don't get entered for a week?"

### 5. **Home Services (HVAC/Plumbing/Electrical)**
- **Target:** Local contractors with 2-5 employees
- **Pain:** Parts invoices, subcontractor bills
- **Approach:** Yelp search, cold call, offer to come by
- **Hook:** "Is bookkeeping keeping you from more billable hours?"

### 6. **Retail Boutiques**
- **Target:** Downtown Roseville shops, Fountains area
- **Pain:** Vendor invoices for inventory
- **Approach:** Shop as customer, strike up conversation with owner
- **Hook:** "How do you handle all the vendor paperwork?"

### 7. **Medical/Dental Offices**
- **Target:** Small practices (1-3 providers) in medical plazas
- **Pain:** Equipment invoices, supply orders
- **Approach:** Call office manager, ask for 5 min
- **Hook:** "Would less paperwork time mean more patient time?"

### 8. **Landscaping/Lawn Services**
- **Target:** Companies with crews, maintenance contracts
- **Pain:** Fuel invoices, equipment repairs, subcontractor bills
- **Approach:** LinkedIn, local business groups
- **Hook:** "Are you losing invoices before they get to your bookkeeper?"

### 9. **Construction/Remodeling Contractors**
- **Target:** Local GCs, kitchen/bath remodelers
- **Pain:** Material invoices from multiple suppliers, change orders
- **Approach:** Home Depot parking lot visits (common meeting spot)
- **Hook:** "What if invoices from suppliers auto-posted to QB?"

### 10. **Professional Services (Legal, CPA, Insurance)**
- **Target:** Small firms (1-5 people) in office parks
- **Pain:** Software subscriptions, professional services
- **Approach:** Chamber of Commerce directory, networking events
- **Hook:** "Is manual invoice entry worth your billable rate?"

---

## **Cold Approach Script**

**Opening:**
> "Hi, I'm [Name]. I'm building a tool that reads invoice emails and posts them straight to QuickBooks — no typing. I'm testing it with local businesses. Mind if I ask how you handle invoices now?"

**Discovery:**
- "How many invoices do you get per week?"
- "Who enters them into QuickBooks?"
- "How long does that take?"
- "Ever lose an invoice before it got entered?"

**The Ask:**
> "Would you be open to trying it for free for a week? I just need you to forward 5-10 invoice emails to a test address and tell me what breaks."
