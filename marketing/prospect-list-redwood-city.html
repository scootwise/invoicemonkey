Cold Approach Script — Redwood City Specific

Opening

"Hi, I'm [Name]. I live in the area and I'm building a tool that reads invoice emails and posts them straight to QuickBooks — no typing. I'm testing it with Redwood City businesses. Mind if I ask how you handle invoices now?"

Discovery Questions

  • "How many invoices do you get per week from [vendors/suppliers]?"
  • "Who enters them into QuickBooks — you or someone else?"
  • "How long does that usually take?"
  • "Ever lose an invoice before it got entered?"
  • "What's your biggest headache with bookkeeping?"
  • "How do you handle invoices that come in by email vs. mail?"

The Ask

"Would you be open to trying it for free for a week? I just need you to forward 5-10 invoice emails to a test address and tell me what breaks. I'm local, so I can come by if you have any questions."

Redwood City Specific Hooks

  • "I know rents are high here — saving time on bookkeeping might help you focus on revenue."
  • "Between serving downtown foot traffic and managing paperwork, where does the time go?"
  • "I noticed a lot of Peninsula businesses struggle with the same thing — invoices piling up."
