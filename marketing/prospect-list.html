Cold Approach Script

Opening

"Hi, I'm [Name]. I'm building a tool that reads invoice emails and posts them straight to QuickBooks — no typing. I'm testing it with local businesses. Mind if I ask how you handle invoices now?"

Discovery Questions

  • "How many invoices do you get per week?"
  • "Who enters them into QuickBooks?"
  • "How long does that take?"
  • "Ever lose an invoice before it got entered?"
  • "What's your biggest headache with bookkeeping?"

The Ask

"Would you be open to trying it for free for a week? I just need you to forward 5-10 invoice emails to a test address and tell me what breaks. No commitment, just feedback."
