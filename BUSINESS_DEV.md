## 1. Target Customer Segments

### Priority 1: Property Management Companies
**Why first:** High invoice volume, pain is acute, QuickBooks users, easy to identify.

**The Pain:**
- Receive 50-200 invoices/month from vendors (maintenance, repairs, cleaning)
- Each invoice takes 5-10 minutes to manually enter
- Property managers are NOT accountants - they hate data entry
- Late payments because invoices sit in email

**The Pitch:** "Process invoices 10x faster. Upload a PDF, we post it to QuickBooks automatically."

**Target Accounts:**
- 20-200 unit property management companies
- Using QuickBooks Online
- 1-5 office staff doing data entry
- In Placer County, Sacramento, Bay Area

**Entry Points:**
- Contact through CREA (California Rental Housing Association)
- Cold outreach via LinkedIn: "I help property managers eliminate invoice data entry"
- Local meetups (property management groups)

---

### Priority 2: Trade Contractors with Office Staff
**Examples:** HVAC, electrical, plumbing companies

**The Pain:**
- Receive invoices from suppliers (materials, parts, equipment)
- Office manager spends hours each week on data entry
- Owner wants to see real-time job costs but data lags

**The Pitch:** "Your office manager spends 10 hours/week on invoice entry. We cut it to 1 hour."

**Target Accounts:**
- $2M-10M revenue
- 2-10 office employees
- Owner involved in daily operations
- QuickBooks users

**Entry Points:**
- Angi/HomeAdvisor contractor lists
- Trade association directories (BPI, ACCA)
- LinkedIn: "HVAC" + "Office Manager" + location

### Priority 3: Restaurants with Multiple Locations

**The Pain:**
- 20-50 invoices/day from food suppliers
- Each location sends invoices to central accounting
- Accounting is buried in manual entry

**The Pitch:** "Forward supplier invoices to process@invoicemonkey.app, they appear in QuickBooks automatically."

**Target Accounts:**

**Entry Points:**
